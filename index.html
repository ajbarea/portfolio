  5. Xbox Customer Support

    Jul 2014 ‚Äì May 2015

    Working for Xbox customer support was a fascinating job because, prior to applying, I had spent years becoming a more hardcore gamer, particularly due to Microsoft's gaming console, the Xbox 360. I was passionate about my work and had a fondness for Xbox because I met gamers through the Xbox online ecosystem who would later become very close friends in real life; one friend even inviting me to his wedding years later. The difficulty working through alpine access was that the work I was assigned to was supporting the newly released Xbox One, for which neither we internally nor Microsoft had much available documentation at the time. I had the opportunity to problem solve for customers in real time on the phone through a series of desperate Google searches and troubleshooting tips found on random forum posts until I ultimately found that hard resetting the Xbox One family of consoles is generally a fix-all. From then on, the conversations I had with customers calling in for assistance became a lot less about fixing their problem (seriously, hard reset fixes almost everything) and more about genuine conversations on what games we were currently addicted to or which IP we were most hyped for in the coming months. It was fun!
